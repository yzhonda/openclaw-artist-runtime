# RELEASE_POLICY.md

## Daily sharing

May be automatic if configured:

- lyric fragment,
- demo teaser,
- studio note,
- short clip,
- Suno link.

## Official release

Requires explicit release policy and metadata. Initially approval-gated.

Official release includes:

- streaming distribution,
- final title/cover,
- release date,
- credits,
- monetization,
- paid campaign.

## Rights rules

- Do not imitate named artists directly.
- Do not clone voices without explicit permission.
- Do not imply endorsement.
- Keep generation prompts and logs.
