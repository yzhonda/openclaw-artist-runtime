# Product Brief

Artist Runtime is an OpenClaw plugin that turns an agent into a public autonomous AI musician.
It is not a prompt helper and not a private studio notebook. It should operate continuously on a Mac,
usually without the producer looking at the screen.

## Core experience

The artist:

1. maintains its own identity and current artistic state;
2. notices themes and develops song ideas;
3. writes lyrics and Suno prompt packs;
4. generates music through Suno;
5. selects takes;
6. creates social assets;
7. publishes to selected platforms;
8. keeps complete prompt and action history.

The producer:

- sets policy;
- connects accounts;
- reviews logs;
- intervenes on exceptional/high-risk cases;
- can pause, recover, or redirect the artist.

## Product default

The public artist premise is fixed. The producer chooses platform selection, budgets, cadence, and authority levels.
